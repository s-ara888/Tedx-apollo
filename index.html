Mission

At TEDxMaadi, it is our purpose to ignite minds, provide voice, and shed light on ideas‚Äîwith the inspiration of Apollo, the Greek god of light, truth, and imagination. This year, we celebrate human innovation in all its forms, including art, science, philosophy, and the intersection of these fields. Through engaging, inspiring, and informative discussions, we aim to foster positive dialogue within and outside our community. TEDxMaadi is dedicated to celebrating the power of storytelling and idea-sharing, lighting the way for spreading ideas of values from Maadi to the world.

Vision

At TEDx Maadi, we envision a future where bold ideas transcend boundaries, where every voice does not merely have an echo, but a source of inspiration. A stage that conflates persuasion with guidance and gathers those who decode silence. Our vision is to bring the light to the world, inspired by the shadowed lines of code, Greek mythology, and the fire that guided ancient journeys into the unknown. From ancient minds to modern ones, we strive to be a stage for inspiring youth and helping them find their selves and their own potentials
